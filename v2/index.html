The golden number — playable, and honest about itselfZłota liczba — grywalna i uczciwa wobec siebie

A 3D dodecahedron is the navigator: its twelve faces open onto chambers about φ — discover it with a slider, watch whirling squares coil into a spiral, fold the five Platonic solids, drive a live quasicrystal shader. Throughout, mathematical rigor is separated from romantic numerology — the nautilus that isn't φ, the Parthenon nobody measured. You earn gold and ranks for the effort.
