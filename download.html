Document Anonymizer.

Anonymizes PDFs, Word docs, text files and images — entirely on your own computer. Nothing leaves the machine.

You're working with a document that contains personal data — a mediation protocol, a psychological report, court papers, a transcript, a candidate's CV, internal correspondence. You need to show it to a third party (a court, a reviewer, a client, an AI) without exposing names, ID numbers, addresses, account numbers.

Anonymizer does this locally: detects sensitive data (names, ID/tax numbers, addresses, e-mails, phone numbers, account numbers, dates of birth, case references) and replaces them with tokens — either neutral pseudonyms or "[ANON]". Works on PDF/DOCX/TXT/images (OCR). Your file never leaves the computer. Inspired by beznazwisk.pl — but local, open, with no page limit.

  • Detection: NER (Bielik 11B v3) + domain rules
  • Formats: PDF, DOCX, TXT, PNG/JPG (OCR)
  • Mediation mode: pseudonyms consistent across docs
  • Audit: change list + mapping file (offline)
  • Batch mode: whole folder in one click
  • Undo: reversible if you keep the mapping
