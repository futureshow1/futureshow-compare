Matila Ghyka's Złota liczba (1931) is one of the great 20th-century treatises on number, proportion, beauty and cosmos. It draws Pythagoras into modern art, the pentagon into the dodecahedron, Plato into Cézanne. It is also, practically speaking, unreadable in sequence.

Sectio Aurea reorganises Ghyka as a building. A golden dodecahedron sits on a starfield. Twelve pentagonal faces, each opening on to a chamber. Seven of them are fully playable. The other five are quiet — for reading, for resting, for losing yourself in the geometry.

01 / Brief

A treatise that refuses linear reading.

For
Architects
Artists
Composers
Anyone curious about Φ

Ghyka is mentioned in design schools, occasionally cited in essays about Le Corbusier or Mondrian, but almost never read end-to-end. The brief was: make a place that does what the book is about — proportion, harmony, beauty as something you walk through and feel — rather than something you read about.

02 / Approach

A solid as the navigator.

Navigator
Dodecahedron (12 faces)
Drag to rotate
Click face to enter

The dodecahedron is the navigator. Twelve faces. Twelve chambers. The whole site is one rotating object — you drag it, you orbit it, you pick a face, you fall into the room behind it.

Seven of the twelve chambers are playable: Number (audio, intervals as ratios), Proportion (drag to discover Φ), Pentada (live GLSL shader of the pentagram), Solids (the five Platonic solids, manipulable), Garden (Fibonacci-driven plants), Lodge (constellation that draws sacred geometry overhead), Probierz (a small myth-game about choosing the golden cut).

Five chambers remain quiet — they hold long-form text (Φ in nature, the five regular solids, the Fibonacci sequence, music intervals, Le Corbusier's Modulor). The book stays present, but it stays in those rooms.

03 / Outcome

Gold, ranks, badges. Quietly.

Progression
Gold coins (Φ-rated rewards)
Ranks (Profan → Adept → ...)
Badges (one per chamber)

Each chamber rewards engagement with gold (Ghyka writes constantly about gold sections, gold rectangles, gold ratios — turning that into in-app currency was the most natural move in the project). Gold unlocks ranks. Ranks unlock badges. Ranks unlock the seventh chamber.

The whole game is gentle. Nothing punitive, nothing competitive, no leaderboard. Just enough structure to give the visitor a reason to come back to a chamber they walked past on the first visit.

04 / Stack

Tools.

Tech
three.js (dodecahedron + shaders)
Web Audio (interval tones)
localStorage progress

three.js for the central dodecahedron and the Platonic solids chamber. Custom GLSL shader for the pentagram chamber. Web Audio for the interval-tone chamber (you can hear a 1:Φ ratio in pitch). All progress persists in localStorage — no account required.

Total weight: heavier than the other flagships (it's a 3D scene), but still ships as a static site with no backend.
